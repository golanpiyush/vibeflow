## ‚ú® What is VibeFlow?

VibeFlow reimagines music listening as a social experience. Built with Flutter, it combines powerful music playback with social features that let you connect with friends through music. Whether you're streaming your favorite tracks, sharing playlists, or discovering what others are listening to, VibeFlow brings people together through the universal language of music.

## üöÄ Getting Started

### Installation

1. Download the latest APK from the [Releases](../../releases) page
2. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" in your Android settings
3. Install the APK
4. Launch VibeFlow and create your account
5. Start exploring music and connecting with friends!

### First Time Setup

1. **Create Account** - Sign up with your email
2. **Optional: Enter Access Code** - Unlock social features (or skip for music-only mode)
3. **Customize Theme** - Choose your preferred theme in Settings
4. **Grant Permissions** - Allow storage access for offline downloads
5. **Start Listening** - Search for your favorite music and enjoy!
